Seeing like a Foundation

This portfolio includes a set of data visualiztions created as a part of a data science bootcamp. While the website will need updates if it will ever serve as a public portfolio, I hope you enjoy my analysis of IRS 990 records (2017) will prove interseting, at least visually. I ambititously (or more accurately precociously) set out to investigate a set of APIs, though the way I went about my project meant looping through a lot of calls, and I got blocked!

In the meantime, I'll present some agreggate data on philanthropic entities in the US, based on IRS Nonprofit financial records. The one above is a riff on a weather data query (temp by latitude), but instead, the total revenue of individual Philanthropic Organizations by Latitude of incorporation. While it might look like there's a slight trend toward revenue and philanthropic activity clusters around 40 degrees, you're mostly seeing where large cities are in the US.

Reviewing the rest of the site, you will find visualizations for:

  • Philanthropy Organization Revenue by Latitude
  • Philanthropic Organizations by Revenue Bracket
  • Philanthropy Revenue by State
  • A measure I term Philanthropic 'Power' by State

Also included in this dashboard are descriptions of observable trends based on the data.
